First of all.

Coachella is a music festival.
And the most famous artists of each year sing there.




























Historical Background

The festival was created in 1997 by Goldenvoice but the opening was canceled for two years.
The first years of Coachella were very difficult because the price of the tickets were very low and the expenses were very high.
Later they had already reached a financial balance and the number of participants was higher year by year























































Time.

The festival was inagurated in 1999.




























Participants.

The festival has an average of 250,000 participants.




























Activities.

The Coachella festival takes place for four days in the month of April.
In Coachella you can do different activities, but the main activity is the music festival.




























Surprising Facts.

The price of the festival went from $50 to the $450 that it costs now.



Stagecoach is also a music festival, and it's always scheduled for the weekend after the last Coachella show. And this festival was also created by Goldenvoice.




























insteresring Facts.

The festival was only canceled two times.

Conclusion.

Coachella it's a well-known festival and if you have the money to pay for it, it's absolutely worth going to.
